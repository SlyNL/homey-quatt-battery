Integrate your Quatt Home Battery with Homey.

This app connects your Quatt Home Battery to Homey, giving you live status, energy insights, and Flow automation support.

Features:
- Live state of charge (%), power (W), and flow direction
- Total and daily savings (€ incl. VAT)
- Energy charged/discharged (kWh)
- Flow triggers: battery above/below threshold, charging, discharging, idle
- Flow conditions: battery is/isn't charging or discharging
- Configurable poll interval and solar capacity

Pairing:
You need the UUID (access key) and serial number from your battery label or the Quatt mobile app.

Note: This app uses the reverse-engineered Quatt mobile API and is not officially supported by Quatt.
