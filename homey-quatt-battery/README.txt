Integrate your Quatt Home Battery with Homey.

This app connects your Quatt Home Battery to Homey, giving you real-time insights into your battery's status, energy flow, and savings — directly in the Homey app and energy overview.

FEATURES
- Live state of charge (%)
- Live power (charging / discharging)
- Power flow direction and control mode
- Daily energy totals: charged, discharged, solar production, house consumption, grid import/export
- Cumulative savings in euros
- Full integration with Homey Energy overview
- Flow cards: trigger automations when battery drops below or rises above a set percentage, or when charging/discharging starts

PAIRING
You need three values from the label on your Quatt Home Battery:
- UUID (access key)
- Serial number
- Check code

NOTES
This is an unofficial integration, not affiliated with or supported by Quatt.
The app uses the Quatt mobile API, reverse engineered by the home-assistant-quatt community.
API changes by Quatt may affect functionality.
